Biography:


I am currently a first-year student at Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) and am majoring in Audio/Visual Technology! I transferred over to BMCC from Brooklyn College, where I pursued a degree in Physical Education before taking time off during the pandemic to work and care for my grandparents.

These days, I work in both the catering industry, and Guest Services Department at Madison Square Garden. There, I found my love and passion for live music! Through work, I've had the opportunity to bring my family to Knicks games, Professional Bull Riding, and concerts featuring Billie Eilish, Michael Bublé, Lizzo, Harry Styles, SZA, and more!!

Although I'm still new to the A/V Tech scene, I want to be able to absorb all the little tid-bits of knowledge and information I possibly can. Maybe one day you'll catch me working front-of-house for your favorite concert!
