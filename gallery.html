Vector Image

My avatar; An abstract representation of myself, created on Pixlr as well. I am most in my element when surrounded by nature, so I used an image of the sun setting over a lake as the starting point of this project.

What a lovely spot to sit and watch the colors of the world pass you by.



GIF Animation

My GIF Animation; Hand-drawn using black sharpie on white index cards.

I used the well known joke, "Why did the chicken cross the road?", as the foundation for this project. I wanted to bring it to life while experimenting with a new technique!


Audio Project

My audio project; Composed of audio clips from Freesound.org., and mixed using Audacity.

My goal was to capture the essence of Puerto Rico during hurricane season, and reflect the environment so many of my family members reside in. Within this project, you hear the rain, wind and Coqui's in the background, while the foreground is commanded by the following features: A FEMA alarm, a military aircraft, and the sound of people struggling to communicate over radio.;[
